Our Mission

FloodGuard exists to safeguard lives and livelihoods by ensuring early preparedness, rapid response, and community resilience.

Why It's Important

Our mission is to empower communities with technology-driven solutions that predict, prepare, and protect against floods. We aim to reduce loss of life, minimize damage, and speed up recovery.

Preparedness helps communities respond faster, rescue people more effectively, and maintain essential supplies during an emergency.

Why Flood Preparedness Matters

Early Preparedness

Actions taken before disasters—like kits, evacuation routes, and family plans—reduce harm and speed recovery.

First Aid

Provides immediate care to injured people and stabilises victims until professionals arrive.

Rescue

Helps plan rescue operations quickly and coordinate responders effectively.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does FloodGuard predict floods?

FloodGuard uses real-time dam levels, rainfall, and inflow/outflow data to forecast potential flood risks.

What should I include in an emergency kit?

Essential items include drinking water, medicines, torch, mobile charger, dry food, and important documents.

How will I get alerts?

Alerts are sent via SMS, email, and the FloodGuard app for registered users.

Is FloodGuard only for cities?

No, it's designed to support both urban and rural communities.
